Virus and Hacks Reference Sheet

Using the information below and the information found in the Data Files, you need to identify which virus out of the four infected Jane Goodall.

Email Viruses

ILOVEYOU

Sends an email with the subject ILOVEYOU with an attachment that claims it is a love letter specifically for you. The attachment often has a .vbs extension.

Melissa Virus

Sends an email with the subject that includes the words “Important message from” or "Urgent" that includes an attachment that has a .vbs extension.

MyDoom

Subject of the emails contains some form of “Error”, “Mail Delivery Test”, or an indication of failure. Often contains in the actual email the line: “Andy; I’m just doing my job, nothing personal, sorry”. Will avoid sending email to .edu addresses.

SoBig

Subject of the email makes it look like it is in response to another email or something important. (Ex. Re: Thank you! Re: Your application, Urgent, Re: Movie). The attached file can have a variety of obscure file extensions.
